Refund Policy

This policy applies to all payments, wallet top-ups, and digital service transactions completed through GETXH.

All payments are final and non-refundable once a payment has been submitted, credited, or processed on the GETXH platform.

01

No Exceptions

GETXH follows a strict no-refund policy. This applies to wallet additions, service purchases, order submissions, and payment confirmations.

Refunds are not provided for change of mind, user error, incorrect entry, duplicate attempts, or dissatisfaction after a valid transaction has been completed.

02

User Responsibility

You are fully responsible for verifying all payment details before completing a transaction — including amount, transaction reference, selected service, link, and quantity.

Any error caused by incorrect user submission remains the sole responsibility of the user.

03

Successful Transaction Rule

If a transaction is successfully completed, it is considered final. Once recorded or acted upon by the platform, the transaction cannot be reversed or refunded.

04

Agreement

By making a payment on GETXH, you confirm that you have reviewed this Refund Policy and expressly agree to the non-refundable nature of all payments.
