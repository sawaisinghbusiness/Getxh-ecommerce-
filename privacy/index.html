Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how GETXH collects, uses, stores, and protects user information when users access the platform, create an account, submit transactions, or use digital services.

Our Core Privacy Commitment

GETXH values user privacy and handles information with strict internal care. We DO NOT sell, share, or distribute your data under any circumstances except where disclosure is legally required by applicable law, lawful government request, or regulatory compliance obligation.

Information We Collect

We collect only basic information reasonably required to operate the platform and complete digital service transactions. This may include:

  • Name and account identity details provided during registration.
  • Email address and login-related account information.
  • Transaction references, payment details submitted by the user, and wallet-related records.
  • Order inputs such as links, quantities, and service-related data entered by the user.
  • Basic technical and usage information required to secure and maintain platform access.

How We Use Information

User information is used only for legitimate operational purposes connected with the GETXH platform. These purposes include:

  • Creating and maintaining user accounts.
  • Processing payments, wallet requests, and service orders.
  • Verifying payment references and transaction submissions.
  • Providing user support and responding to account-related queries.
  • Improving service flow, transaction handling, and platform reliability.
  • Preventing fraud, abuse, unauthorized access, and operational misuse.

Data Sharing Restriction

GETXH does not engage in selling user data, renting user data, marketing user data, or distributing user data to unrelated third parties. User information is not monetized. Any information used for payment processing, technical hosting, or core platform operation is handled only to the extent reasonably necessary for those services to function.

Security Practices

We apply strong and practical security practices to reduce the risk of unauthorized access, misuse, or exposure of data. These measures may include secure authentication methods, restricted administrative access, system-level protections, transaction verification controls, and reasonable monitoring for suspicious activity.

While no digital system can guarantee absolute security, GETXH takes privacy and account protection seriously and aims to use commercially reasonable safeguards for the nature of the platform.

User Data Control

Users are responsible for maintaining the accuracy of the information they submit on the platform. Users should avoid sharing login credentials and should review account and transaction information carefully before submission.

Where applicable, users may contact GETXH to request correction of clearly inaccurate account information stored in the platform. Operational, payment, and transaction records may still be retained as reasonably necessary for compliance, dispute review, fraud prevention, and internal recordkeeping.

Policy Acceptance

By using GETXH, creating an account, or submitting any payment or order, the user acknowledges that they have read and accepted this Privacy Policy. Continued use of the platform constitutes ongoing agreement to this policy.
