Privacy Policy

This policy explains how GETXH collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our platform.

Core Commitment — GETXH does not sell, share, or distribute your personal data under any circumstances, except where legally required.

01

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information required to operate the platform:

  • Name and account identity provided during registration
  • Email address and login-related account details
  • Transaction references, payment details, and wallet records
  • Order inputs such as links, quantities, and service data
  • Basic technical information required to secure platform access
02

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used strictly for operational purposes:

  • Creating and maintaining your account
  • Processing payments, wallet requests, and service orders
  • Verifying payment references and transaction submissions
  • Providing support and responding to account queries
  • Preventing fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
03

Data Sharing

GETXH does not sell, rent, or distribute your data to third parties. Any information used for payment processing or technical hosting is handled only to the extent necessary.

04

Security

We use secure authentication, restricted admin access, transaction verification controls, and activity monitoring to protect your data.

05

Your Data Control

Contact us at agency@getxh.in to request correction of inaccurate account data. Operational and payment records may be retained for compliance and dispute resolution.

06

Policy Acceptance

By using GETXH or creating an account, you confirm that you have read and accepted this Privacy Policy.
