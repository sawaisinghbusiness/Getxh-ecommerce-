Cancellation Policy

This Cancellation Policy explains when cancellation may or may not be available for orders placed on GETXH.

General Cancellation Rule

Cancellation is NOT available for all orders. Cancellation may be available only for certain specific or special orders where cancellation is expressly stated in the service description at the time of ordering.

Read Service Description Carefully

Users must read every service description carefully before placing an order. If cancellation is not explicitly mentioned for a service, the user must assume that the order is non-cancellable.

No Cancellation After Processing

Once an order has been submitted, accepted, queued, initiated, or otherwise processed in any form, cancellation is not permitted. Orders already under execution cannot be stopped, withdrawn, or reversed.

Platform Decision Authority

GETXH reserves the sole and final right to determine whether an order qualifies for cancellation. Any decision made by the platform regarding cancellation eligibility shall be final and binding.

User Responsibility

Users are responsible for checking order details, service description, quantity, pricing, and link accuracy before submission. Orders placed in error due to user negligence do not create any cancellation right.
